Chapter 1

Running from the Shadows

It was wednesday when I first saw it.

I had been walking home when it caught my eye. A shadow passing by the window in the attic. I told myself I was just imagining it. But I knew what I saw.

I didn't react. I didn't even flinch. But I knew it saw me too.

I noticed a note on the front doorstep. it read:

Why won't you leave me alone?

I crumpled up the paper and tossed it in the bushes beside me. I wasn't interested in it's games.

I had things that I needed to do.

  1. Find her
  2. Destroy the evidence
    • Leave nothing behind
